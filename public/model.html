Activation Routine Details

Title
Activation Routine 1: Morning Metabolism Boost Flow
Goal
Wake up your body, increase circulation, and set your metabolism for the day
Duration
5–7 minutes
Steps

Standing Reach & Side Stretch 1 minuto Stand tall, feet hip-width apart. Reach both arms overhead, stretch up. Lean gently to one side, hold for 10 sec, switch sides.

Shoulder Rolls 30 segundos Roll shoulders forward in slow circles for 15 sec. Then roll backward for 15 sec.

Torso Twist 1 minuto Place hands on your hips or extend arms outward. Gently twist your upper body left and right, keeping hips stable.

Knee Lifts with Arm Reach 1 minuto March in place, lifting knees to hip level. Reach opposite arm to the sky as each knee lifts.

Forward Fold & Breathe 1 minuto Stand tall, exhale and slowly fold forward. Let your arms hang, bend knees slightly. Take 3–5 deep breaths, slowly rise.

Spine Roll-Up & Posture Reset 1 minuto From standing, tuck your chin, roll your spine down slowly. Then roll back up, stacking vertebrae, ending with a posture reset: shoulders back, chest open.
